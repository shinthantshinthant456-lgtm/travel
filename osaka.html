Explore Osaka, Japan

Overview

Osaka is a city on the island of Honshu, Japan, known for its modern architecture, vibrant nightlife, and delicious street food. It is home to famous attractions such as Osaka Castle, Universal Studios Japan, and the neon-lit Dotonbori district. Often called the “Nation’s Kitchen,” Osaka is a center of Japanese food culture, offering local specialties like takoyaki and okonomiyaki.

Top Attractions

  • Osaka Castle: Historic 16th-century castle surrounded by gardens
  • Dotonbori: Neon lights, street food, and nightlife by the canal
  • Universal Studios Japan (USJ): Theme park with Harry Potter & Nintendo World
  • Umeda Sky Building: Futuristic skyscraper with floating garden observatory
  • Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan: One of the world’s largest aquariums
